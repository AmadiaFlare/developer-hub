**Flare is the blockchain for data ☀️**, empowering developers with secure, decentralized access to reliable and verifiable data from other blockchains and the internet. 
Flare uniquely integrates specialized data protocols at its core, making it the only EVM-compatible Layer-1 blockchain specifically optimized for decentralized access to price feeds, blockchain states, and external APIs.

By providing broad data access at scale and minimal cost, Flare delivers a full-stack solution for building the next generation of secure, interoperable, and data-driven decentralized applications.
